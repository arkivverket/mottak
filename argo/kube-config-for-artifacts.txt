
Example config for argo artifactRepository. This will enable argo to store logs as objects in the objectstore
To configure a new k8s instance issue the following:

$ kubectl edit configmap workflow-controller-configmap -n argo

and enter the below config. Adapt to your use.





data:
  config: |
    artifactRepository:
      s3:
        bucket:    pilot-spool
        keyPrefix: log-
        endpoint: storage.googleapis.com
        accessKeySecret:
          name: s3-cred
          key: accessKeySecret
        secretKeySecret:
          name: s3-cred
          key: secretKeySecret
